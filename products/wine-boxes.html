Wine Boxes Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating premium wine boxes that combine elegance with functionality. Our range includes 1-panel hinged boxes, 4-panel book style boxes, and hinged boxes, each offering unique presentation options for your wine products. We use high-quality materials such as silver and gold mylar papers, synthetic leather, gloss art, greyboard, and MDF, with various finishing options like gold foil stamping, matt lamination, and embossing. Our wine boxes are perfect for retail packaging, with options for full-color 4C printing, white mask, and special features like inner boxes, EVA foam, and velvet inserts.
