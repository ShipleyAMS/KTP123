Gift Box Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating high-quality, customizable gift boxes that combine elegance with functionality. Our range of box types includes telescope boxes, book style boxes, hinged boxes, display boxes, and drawer boxes, each offering unique presentation options for your products. We use premium materials such as 157gsm gloss art, 400gsm C1S, and various specialty papers to ensure durability and a professional finish. Our gift boxes are perfect for corporate gifts, retail packaging, or special occasions, with options for full-color 4C printing, PMS colors, and various finishing techniques like matt lamination, gold foil stamping, and spot UV.
