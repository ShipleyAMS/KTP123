Jewelry Box Production

At King Tai, we specialize in creating exquisite, high-quality jewelry boxes that combine elegance with functionality. Our range of box types includes double door boxes, hinged boxes, lid & base boxes, book style boxes, and more, each offering unique presentation options for your jewelry pieces. We use premium materials such as 157gsm gloss art, special papers, greyboard, and luxurious finishes like velvet and EVA foam to ensure both protection and a premium presentation. Our jewelry boxes are perfect for retail packaging, corporate gifts, or special occasions, with options for full-color 4C printing, PMS colors, and various finishing techniques like gold foil stamping, embossing, and textured finishes.
