Calendar Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating high-quality, customizable calendars that combine functionality with elegant design. Our range of calendar types includes wood easel stands, metal wiro bindings, telescope boxes, and 4-panel designs, each offering unique presentation options for your content. We use premium materials such as 350gsm C1S and C2S paper, 150gsm craft paper, and 400gsm C1S art paper to ensure durability and a professional finish. Our calendars are perfect for corporate gifts, promotional items, or personal use, with options for full-color 4C printing and various finishing techniques like matt lamination and machine varnishing.
