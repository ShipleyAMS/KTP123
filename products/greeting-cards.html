Greeting Cards Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating premium greeting cards that combine elegance with functionality. Our range includes cards with envelopes, message cards, and polybags, each offering unique presentation options for your products. We use high-quality materials such as 400gsm C2S art card, 157gsm gloss art paper, 140gsm woodfree, and greyboard, with various finishing options like matt lamination and gold foil stamping. Our greeting cards are perfect for retail packaging, with options for full color 4C printing, and special features like diecut, fold, and assembly.
