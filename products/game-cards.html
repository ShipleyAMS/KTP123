Game Card Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating high-quality, customizable game cards, puzzles, and educational materials that combine functionality with engaging design. Our range of products includes family game cards, study cards, learning cards, jigsaw puzzles, and tarot cards, each offering unique opportunities for entertainment and education. We use premium materials such as 400gsm C2S art card, 157gsm gloss art, and 350gsm C1S to ensure durability and a professional finish. Our products are perfect for family entertainment, educational purposes, or personal use, with options for full-color 4C printing and various finishing techniques like matt lamination and rounded corners.
