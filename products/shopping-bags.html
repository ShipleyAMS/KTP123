Shopping Bags Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating premium shopping bags that combine elegance with functionality. Our range includes bags with paper handles, cotton handles, ribbon handles, and tags, each offering unique presentation options for your products. We use high-quality materials such as 210gsm C1S art paper, 210gsm special paper, and craft paper, with various finishing options like matt lamination, gold foil stamping, machine varnishing, and embossing. Our shopping bags are perfect for retail packaging, with options for full color 4C printing, 1C printing, and 1 PMS + 1C printing, and special features like diecut, glued, and assembly.
