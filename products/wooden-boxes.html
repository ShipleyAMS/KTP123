Wooden Boxes Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating premium wooden boxes that combine elegance with functionality. Our range includes 2-piece hinged boxes, 2-layer wooden boxes, hinged wooden boxes, and boxes with paper designs, each offering unique presentation options for your products. We use high-quality materials such as 10mm wood, velvet inserts, metal locks, and paper cards, with various finishing options like gold foil stamping, debossing, silkscreen, and varnishing. Our wooden boxes are perfect for retail packaging, with options for PMS and 1C production, and special features like inserts, handles, locks, and pouch holes.
