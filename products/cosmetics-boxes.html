Cosmetic Boxes Production Services

At King Tai, we specialize in creating premium cosmetics boxes that combine elegance with functionality. Our range includes hinged boxes, double door boxes, lid & base boxes, and panel boxes, each offering unique presentation options for your cosmetics products. We use high-quality materials such as white craft paper, gloss art, mylar papers, and greyboard, with various finishing options like gold foil stamping, matt lamination, and embossing. Our cosmetics boxes are perfect for retail packaging, with options for full-color 4C printing, PMS colors, and special features like inserts, blisters, and mirrors.
